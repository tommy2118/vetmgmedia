Questions Contractors Ask

Will this website generate leads for me?

Not right away—and that's not the point. Our sites aren't built to chase leads. They're built to qualify you for financing, establish trust with referrers, and help your existing leads convert when they check you out.

Your job is to hustle leads (word-of-mouth, referrals, door-knocking). Our job is to make sure you look pro when customers and lenders check you out online.

Eventually? Yes, your site can become a lead source through Google searches and organic traffic. But that's months 6-12, not day one.

How do I know if I'll qualify for financing programs after working with you?

We can't guarantee approval (lenders make final decisions), but we know what they're looking for. If you're close to meeting requirements but need a professional website and trust signals, we can help you get there.

Which financing companies do you work with?

We're familiar with major contractor financing programs. We provide guidance on which programs might fit your business type, revenue, and time in business. We can also integrate financing widgets and application forms directly into your site.

What if I already have a website but was rejected by financing companies?

Perfect - that's exactly who our Touch-Up/Redo package is for. We'll review your existing site, identify what's keeping you from qualifying, and fix it. Often it's missing trust signals, outdated design, poor mobile experience, or lack of professional polish.

How much more revenue can I expect from offering financing?

Industry data shows contractors who offer financing close 30-40% more jobs and increase average project size by 20-30%. Customers are more likely to say yes to bigger projects when they can finance them. Exact numbers depend on your industry and market.

Do I own the website or am I renting it?

You OWN it. We build it, hand over all files, and include 1 year of hosting. After that, you can host it yourself anywhere or continue with us for $120/year. No lock-in. No monthly fees.

Which financing platforms should I apply to?

Different platforms have different requirements. GreenSky needs 2+ years and $1M+ revenue. PowerPay and FTL Finance accept startups. Wisetack caps loans at $15k. Enhancify goes up to $200k.

Use our free Platform Matcher tool to see which of the 7 major platforms you qualify for based on your years in business, revenue, credit, and project size.

You'll get: Personalized matches, side-by-side fee comparison, ROI calculator, and exact next steps for each platform.

Do you provide guidance on applying to financing programs?

We'll guide you through the application process. We know what information lenders need, what they're looking for, and how to present your business in the best light. We're not loan brokers, but we've helped dozens of contractors get approved.

Can I offer financing even if I have poor credit?

It depends. Most financing companies care more about your business credit and operational history than personal credit. If you've been in business 1-3 years, have steady revenue, and meet basic requirements, you can often qualify even with less-than-perfect personal credit. Some financing partners have lower credit requirements than others. We can provide guidance on which programs might be a good fit based on your situation.

What is a business credit profile?

Business credit is separate from your personal credit. It's based on your company's payment history with suppliers, lenders, and vendors. Financing companies often check your business credit (through Dun & Bradstreet, Experian Business, or Equifax Business) to see if your company pays bills on time. If you've been in business for a while and pay your suppliers consistently, you likely have some business credit history even if you haven't formally built it.
